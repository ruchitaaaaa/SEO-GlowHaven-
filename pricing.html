Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common queries about our unisex parlour services

What services do you offer at your unisex parlour?

We offer a wide range of services including hair styling, beard styling, facial treatments, manicure & pedicure, makeup, and waxing. Our services are tailored to suit the needs of both men and women.

Do you provide bridal makeup services?

Yes, we specialize in bridal makeup services. Our professional makeup artists will ensure you look stunning on your special day with customized makeup packages to suit your preferences.

How long does a typical session at your parlour last?

The duration of a session depends on the service you choose. Generally, our sessions range from 30 minutes to 2 hours, depending on the service and your requirements.

Do you offer any discounts or special packages?

Yes, we offer various discounts and special packages throughout the year. Keep an eye on our website and social media channels for updates on our latest offers.

Can I book an appointment online?

Absolutely! You can easily book your appointment online through our website. Simply visit the booking section and choose your desired service, date, and time slot.
